# Scarm, Malebranche of the Burning Abyss

- **Card type:** Effect Monster
- **Attribute:** DARK
- **Monster type:** Fiend
- **Type line:** Fiend / Effect
- **Level:** 3
- **ATK:** 800
- **DEF:** 2000

## Card text

If you control a monster that is not a "Burning Abyss" monster, destroy this card. You can only use 1 of these effects of "Scarm, Malebranche of the Burning Abyss" per turn, and only once that turn.
●If you control no Spells/Traps: You can Special Summon this card from your hand.
●During the End Phase, if this card is in the GY because it was sent there this turn: You can add 1 Level 3 DARK Fiend monster from your Deck to your hand, except "Scarm, Malebranche of the Burning Abyss".
