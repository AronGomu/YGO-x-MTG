# Reeshaddoll Wendi

- **Card type:** Effect Monster
- **Attribute:** WIND
- **Monster type:** Psychic
- **Type line:** Psychic / Flip / Effect
- **Level:** 3
- **ATK:** 1500
- **DEF:** 1000

## Card text

FLIP: You can Special Summon 1 "Shaddoll" monster from your Deck in face-up or face-down Defense Position, except "Reeshaddoll Wendi".
If this card is sent to the GY by card effect: You can Special Summon 1 "Shaddoll" monster from your Deck in face-down Defense Position, except "Reeshaddoll Wendi". You can only use 1 "Reeshaddoll Wendi" effect per turn, and only once that turn.
