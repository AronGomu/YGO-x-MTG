# Shaddoll Squamata

- **Card type:** Effect Monster
- **Attribute:** DARK
- **Monster type:** Spellcaster
- **Type line:** Spellcaster / Flip / Effect
- **Level:** 4
- **ATK:** 1800
- **DEF:** 1000

## Card text

FLIP: You can target 1 monster on the field; destroy it.
If this card is sent to the GY by a card effect: You can send 1 "Shaddoll" card from your Deck to the GY, except "Shaddoll Squamata". You can only use 1 "Shaddoll Squamata" effect per turn, and only once that turn.
