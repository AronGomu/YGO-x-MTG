# Shaddoll Hedgehog

- **Card type:** Effect Monster
- **Attribute:** DARK
- **Monster type:** Spellcaster
- **Type line:** Spellcaster / Flip / Effect
- **Level:** 3
- **ATK:** 800
- **DEF:** 200

## Card text

FLIP: You can add 1 "Shaddoll" Spell/Trap from your Deck to your hand.
If this card is sent to the GY by a card effect: You can add 1 "Shaddoll" monster from your Deck to your hand, except "Shaddoll Hedgehog". You can only use 1 "Shaddoll Hedgehog" effect per turn, and only once that turn.
