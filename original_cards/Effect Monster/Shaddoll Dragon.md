- **Monster type:** Spellcaster
- **Type line:** Spellcaster / Flip / Effect

## Card text

FLIP: You can target 1 card your opponent controls; return it to the hand.
If this card is sent to the GY by card effect: You can target 1 Spell/Trap on the field; destroy it. You can only use 1 "Shaddoll Dragon" effect per turn, and only once that turn.
