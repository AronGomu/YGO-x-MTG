# Cir, Malebranche of the Burning Abyss

- **Card type:** Effect Monster
- **Attribute:** DARK
- **Monster type:** Fiend
- **Type line:** Fiend / Effect
- **Level:** 3
- **ATK:** 1600
- **DEF:** 1200

## Card text

If you control a monster that is not a "Burning Abyss" monster, destroy this card. You can only use 1 of these effects of "Cir, Malebranche of the Burning Abyss" per turn, and only once that turn.
●If you control no Spells/Traps: You can Special Summon this card from your hand.
●If this card is sent to the GY: You can target 1 "Burning Abyss" monster in your GY, except "Cir, Malebranche of the Burning Abyss"; Special Summon it.
