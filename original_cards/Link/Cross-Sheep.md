# Cross-Sheep

- **Card type:** Link Monster
- **Attribute:** EARTH
- **Monster type:** Beast
- **Type line:** Beast / Link / Effect
- **Link Rating:** 2
- **Link Arrows:** Bottom-Left, Bottom-Right
- **ATK:** 700
- **DEF:** —

## Card text

2 monsters with different names
If a monster is Special Summoned to a zone this card points to (except during the Damage Step): You can apply the following effects, in sequence, based on the card types of the monster(s) this card points to.
●Ritual: Draw 2 cards, then discard 2 cards.
●Fusion: Special Summon 1 Level 4 or lower monster from your GY.
●Synchro: All monsters you control gain 700 ATK.
●Xyz: All monsters your opponent controls lose 700 ATK.
You can only use this effect of "Cross-Sheep" once per turn.
