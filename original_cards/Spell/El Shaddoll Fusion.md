# El Shaddoll Fusion

- **Card type:** Spell Card
- **Property:** Quick-Play

## Card text

Fusion Summon 1 "Shaddoll" Fusion Monster from your Extra Deck, using monsters from your hand or field. You can only activate 1 "El Shaddoll Fusion" per turn.
