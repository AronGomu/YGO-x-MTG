# Shaddoll Fusion

- **Card type:** Spell Card
- **Property:** Normal

## Card text

Fusion Summon 1 "Shaddoll" Fusion Monster from your Extra Deck, using monsters from your hand or field. If your opponent controls a monster that was Special Summoned from the Extra Deck, you can also use monsters in your Deck as material. You can only activate 1 "Shaddoll Fusion" per turn.
