# Spellbook of Secrets

- **Card type:** Spell Card
- **Property:** Normal

## Card text

Add 1 "Spellbook" card from your Deck to your hand, except "Spellbook of Secrets". You can only activate 1 "Spellbook of Secrets" per turn.
