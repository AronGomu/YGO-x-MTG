# Sinister Shadow Games

- **Card type:** Trap Card
- **Property:** Normal

## Card text

Send 1 "Shaddoll" card from your Deck to the GY, then you can change any number of face-down Defense Position "Shaddoll" monsters you control to face-up Defense Position.
