# Mysterion the Dragon Crown

- **Card type:** Fusion Monster
- **Attribute:** LIGHT
- **Monster type:** Spellcaster
- **Type line:** Spellcaster / Fusion / Effect
- **Level:** 8
- **ATK:** 3000
- **DEF:** 1500

## Card text

1 Spellcaster monster + 1 Dragon monster
Cannot be used as Fusion Material. Loses 100 ATK for each of your banished cards. If a monster uses its activated effect to Special Summon itself, or a monster(s) with its same original Type (except during the Damage Step): You can target 1 of those Special Summoned monsters; banish that monster, also banish all monsters from the field with its same original Type. You can only use this effect of "Mysterion the Dragon Crown" once per turn.
