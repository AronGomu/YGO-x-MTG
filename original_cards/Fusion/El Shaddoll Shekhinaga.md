# El Shaddoll Shekhinaga

- **Card type:** Fusion Monster
- **Attribute:** EARTH
- **Monster type:** Machine
- **Type line:** Machine / Fusion / Effect
- **Level:** 10
- **ATK:** 2600
- **DEF:** 3000

## Card text

1 "Shaddoll" monster + 1 EARTH monster
Must first be Fusion Summoned. When a Special Summoned monster activates its effect while you have a "Shaddoll" card in your hand (Quick Effect): You can negate the activation, and if you do, destroy that monster, then send 1 "Shaddoll" card from your hand to the GY. You can only use this effect of "El Shaddoll Shekhinaga" once per turn. If this card is sent to the GY: You can target 1 "Shaddoll" Spell/Trap in your GY; add it to your hand.
