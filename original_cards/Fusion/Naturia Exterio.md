# Naturia Exterio

- **Card type:** Fusion Monster
- **Attribute:** EARTH
- **Monster type:** Beast
- **Type line:** Beast / Fusion / Effect
- **Level:** 10
- **ATK:** 2800
- **DEF:** 2400

## Card text

"Naturia Beast" + "Naturia Barkion"
A Fusion Summon of this card can only be done with the above Fusion Materials. When a Spell/Trap Card is activated (Quick Effect): You can banish 1 card from your GY, then send the top card of your Deck to the GY; negate the activation, and if you do, destroy that card. This card must be face-up on the field to activate and to resolve this effect.
