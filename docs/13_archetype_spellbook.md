# 13 - Archetype: Spellbook

## Alternative costs and Spell Affinity

**Book Affinity** is retired. A creature that used it prints its original alternative-cost text in full on an unnumbered line before abilities:

`**Alternative Cost** — If you **Cast** 1 non-Creature *“Spellbook”* this turn, you may **Cast** this Spell without paying its mana cost.`

Do not grant this alternative cost from a card's name or type. Card-specific alternative costs print their complete payment after **Alternative Cost**.

**Spell Affinity** is a printed, opt-in keyword for *“Spellbook”* Sorceries: “If you control 1 *“Spellbook”* creature, you may **Cast** this spell without paying its mana cost.” A player may use this alternative cost only once each turn. A *“Spellbook”* Sorcery without the printed keyword does not have this alternative cost.

## Card source of truth

Card-by-card values for this archetype exist only in `MSE_projects/13_YGO_Spellbook.mse-set/`. This document retains only archetype-specific alternative-cost rules.
